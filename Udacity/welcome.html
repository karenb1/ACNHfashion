About Me

My name is Karen and I am learning how to code to become a front-end web developer. I currently work as an advanced dental therapist in St. Paul, MN. This means I work with teeth and get to be very artistic making fillings look lovely!
The image in fig. 1 is of my character in Animal Crossing: New Horizon. She is wearing an outfit very similar to what I wear at work!
